About Emma

A passionate UX/UI designer dedicated to creating meaningful digital experiences that make a difference.

Hello! I'm Emma, born and raised in Hong Kong. After graduating from college, I worked in various fields including testing, inspection, and finance. A few years ago, I spent 2 years in Japan, where my experiences and the people I met inspired me to pursue a career in design and digital media.

Currently, I'm studying interactive media design in Ottawa, focusing on becoming a UX designer. I believe design should always focus on people, which is why I'm passionate about creating user-centered solutions that solve real problems and provide delightful experiences.
