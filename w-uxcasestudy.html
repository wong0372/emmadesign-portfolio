TravelGigs

A comprehensive UX case study for a travel app designed for working travelers

Duration 8 weeks
Team 3 UX Designers
Role UX Researcher & Designer
View Prototype See Process

The Problem

Different countries have various regulations for working while traveling, such as working holiday visas that permit travelers to work or exchange services. However, finding a suitable workspace can be challenging, and information may be scattered, especially for unique job types like cultural exchange positions.

73% of travelers struggle to find work opportunities
45% spend weeks searching for suitable positions
68% rely on scattered online resources
